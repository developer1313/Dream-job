At a senior/leadership level, interviews are less about answering questions and more about how you position your experience, decisions, and leadership. The conversation often goes beyond your CV and focuses on how you think, how you lead, and the value you bring to an organization. Our Interview Prep is designed to help you approach these discussions with clarity, structure, and confidence.

We work with you through structured sessions based on real hiring scenarios at leadership level. The focus is on refining how you present your experience, articulate your impact, and handle strategic conversations with senior stakeholders. You will gain clarity on how to position your achievements, navigate complex questions, and maintain control of the discussion. The outcome is simple, you walk into interviews with a clear narrative, strong presence, and the confidence to engage at a senior level.

What's included
✓ Structured sessions modelled on real leadership interviews
✓ Sharpened narrative around impact and decision-making
✓ Strategies for handling stakeholder & board-level conversations
✓ Personalised feedback and replay drills
